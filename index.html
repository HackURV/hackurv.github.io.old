50 Hackers. 12 hours. Awesome chatrooms & workshops

Local Hack Day is a 12 hour Hack Day on your school's campus that brings together the local hacker community to celebrate building awesome technology. On December 3rd 2016, other students around the world will be simultaneously hosting their own Local Hack Day at their school, resulting in the single largest day of student hacking ever. Throughout the day, there will be awesome chatrooms, and fun activities that you can use to meet students in your local community as well as across the globe.

FAQ

  • WHAT IS A LOCALHACKDAY?

    A hackathon is an invention marathon. We provide chatrooms, workshops & mentors can help you hacking what you love. It’s very beginner friendly.

  • HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

    Nothing. HackURV is free. We will provide you food, swag and workspace.

  • WHERE IS THE EVENT?

    The event is being hosted at URV's Campus Catalunya in Tarragona.

    Map

  • DO I NEED TO BE A STUDENT TO ATTEND?

    No. Students who are currently enrolled at a college/university or vocational training are eligible to attend.

  • WHAT IS MAJOR LEAGUE HACKING?

    Major League Hacking is the official student hackathon league in North America & Europe. We work with over 200 Member Events and empower over 70,000 students every year.

  • IS THERE A CODE OF CONDUCT?

    Yes there is. tl;dr: We enforce it very strongly. You can find it here.

    Code of conduct

  • DO I HAVE TO ATTEND THE WHOLE DAY?

    No, you can attend the hours you want. You will be welcomed anytime!

  • BEING A HIGHSCHOOL'S STUDENT, DO I NEED TO GO WITH A TEACHER?

    No, you can come by yourself. Wether you come with the school or not, everyone must be inscribed individually.

  • BEING UNDER 18, DO I NEED A PARENT'S PERMISSION?

    No, you can come by yourself. Remember to register.
